About Me

I am Ameet Pokhrel, a passionate and budding developer from Tehrathum, Nepal. Currently, I am pursuing my Bachelor's degree in BSc (Hons) Computing at Itahari International College. With a strong enthusiasm for technology, I am dedicated to mastering a wide range of skills and tools to create innovative solutions for real-world challenges.

My technical journey encompasses various programming languages and frameworks including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Node.js, Flutter, and Firebase. I'm equally comfortable working with both Linux and Windows operating systems. Beyond coding, I have developed strong interests in graphic design, UI/UX design, cybersecurity, and IoT development, complemented by my passion for photography.

My vision is to leverage cutting-edge technologies to develop impactful solutions. I specialize in creating intuitive web designs, developing mobile applications, and building IoT solutions for smart and connected systems. As a UI designer and photographer, I bring a creative perspective to my technical projects, ensuring user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing outcomes.

Alongside my academic journey at Itahari International College, I am continuously exploring new tools, refining my skills, and working on projects that blend creativity with technology. My multifaceted interests in programming, design, and emerging technologies drive me to push boundaries and innovate.
